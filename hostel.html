WCE Hostel


Hostel for students who are studying in WCE is at beautiful location. Surrounded by the greenary and mountains.

There are separate hostels for Boys and Girls. Girls hostel is located at opposite location to Boys hostel. There is a common play ground for both girls and boys, separate ground for handicaped students.

Security for the hostel is very strict. No one except hostel students can enter into the hostel without legel permissions.

Hoetel is protected by tall walls hence no one can enter into the compound from outside especially the animals from nearest hills.




*Facilities Provided In Hostel*


1) Well furnished rooms for students of WCE


2) Good electric power and water supply.


3) Good food management.


4) Good medical treatement at any serious condition.


5) Gym for students of WCE.


6) Fitness programs for students.




Hostel meal



1) 8:00am-9:00am :- Morning Breakfast.


Breakfast consist of bread, butter, pav, poha, upama etc.



2) 12:00pm-1:30pm :- Lunch.


Lunch consist of chapati, sabji, daal-rice, pulav, etc. sabji is fixed for particular day.



3) 8:30pm-10:00pm :-Dinner


Dinner consist of chapati/roti, chicken/sabji, daal-rice.


Other than the three meals if student want to buy something to eat then also rates of food are affordable in hostel's canteen.
