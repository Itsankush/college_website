Library Of WCE


"The heart of WCE"


Librarian: Mr. Ankush Fartale


   Our college has an excellent library containing a very large number of books, periodicals and journals. It offers the students all sort of facilities. The ground floor provides seating arrangement for about 75 teachers and students.

   The reading hall on the first floor can accomodate about 200 students. The library has book bank facility, run exclusively for the deserving students, especially for needy and deserving students coming from socially-economically weaker sections of society.

   A new text book bank has been established under the college grants commision's scheme. The Reading Hall Is Kept Open From 8:00am to 5:00pm Every Day.

   Our Library, rich in treasure of knowledge, houses a total of 1,08,826 books, 98 periodicals and 51 encyclopedia with computer facility, especially for the needy and deserving students coming from socially-economically weaker sections of society. The internet facility is available to students as well as staff members.

   Library facility is also made availbale to researchers, post graduate students, those preparing for public examinations like UPSC, MPSC and book lovers.



Total No. Of Books- 1,08,828
Periodicals- 32


Library Rules:


1) The library remains open from 8:00am to 5:00pm on all working days.


2) Maintain silence in the library.


3) Books can be issude from the library between the folloeing timings: Monday to Friday: 8:30am to 4:00pm


4) For the reference in the main library and reading hall, books and periodicals can be issued between 8:00 to 4:00pm.


5) Home issue section books can be issued for a period of week after which a fine of Rs. 5/- per day will be charged.


6) If a book is lost by members, it should be replaced or else pay double the price of the book.
