About Wisdom College



  The Wisdom College Of Engineering was established in December 1996. It was the first college in Mumbai to be started by three guys from Mumbai. It was declared open fromally by Mr. Ankush Fartale. Then the Governor of Bombay, on 20th december 1996. A large amount of credit of this institution goes to Mr. Aniket P. Bhagat and Mr. Mahesh U. Behera, who extended a handsome donation of Land and Rs. 5,00,000 from the Wisdom Charitable Trust. In recognition of this gesture, Mr. Ankush Fartale named the institution- Wisdom College Of Engineering
