Sports



Director of Sports: Prof. A. P. Fartale

Establishment of WCE Gymkhana, 20th Dec 1996.



List of sport events students participate:


  Chess, Table tennis, Badminton, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Wrestling, Boxing, Judo, Mallakhamb, Volly ball, Athletics, Water polo, Carrom, Weight-lifting, Power-lifting, Taikwando, Karate, Shooting, Cycling, Cricket, Tug of War, Cross Country, Yo Yo, Gymnastic, Aquatics, Football, Half Marathon.


Incentives and facilities availvle to sportperson:


Sports talent considered for admissions.


Prizes are given to students to encourage them.


Prizes: WCE Shree Best Physique, WCE Kesari Best Wrestier, Best sportsmen prize


Incentives: Students are also given incentives in the form of track suits, trophies, kit bags and Medals.


Scholarship: Govt. of India awarda a Sports Talent Search Scholarship of RS 9,000/- Annually, Freeship, Sports Scholarship.


Grace Marks: At least 60-70 students get benefit of 10 grace marks due toe sports every year as per univercity ordinance.


Special Coaching: Students get benefit of coaching from eminent sport personalities.
